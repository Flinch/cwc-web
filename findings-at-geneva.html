PRESENTATION OF THE FINDINGS AT GENEVA

February 26, 2025

Our Head of Research, Dr. Yashua Alkali Hamza, at the University of Geneva meeting to discuss the draft guidance on "Understanding and Measuring Community Engagement Impacts".

The agenda covered:

  • IFRC's Community Engagement and Accountability Impact framework
  • Draft CE Evaluation Guidance
  • Group review and feedback

The facilitation team included experts from the University of Geneva, IFRC, London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine, and other institutions.
